### 保研/考研

- 常年招收直博/普博以及专业硕士，欢迎面向机器人、人工智能、计算机、自动化、应用数学等学科的同学申请，欢迎邮件咨询。
- We welcome applications from prospective Ph.D. students, including both direct-entry Ph.D. applicants and applicants entering after a master’s degree, as well as professional master’s students. Applicants with backgrounds in robotics, artificial intelligence, computer science, automation, applied mathematics, and related fields are encouraged to apply. Email inquiries are welcome.

### 本研/实习生

- 常年招收本硕博参与 线上/线下 实习，欢迎面向机器人、人工智能、计算机、自动化、应用数学等学科的同学申请。可邮件联系，标题注明[本研]或[实习生]。
- We welcome undergraduate, master’s, and Ph.D. students to join us as online or on-site interns throughout the year. Applicants with backgrounds in robotics, artificial intelligence, computer science, automation, applied mathematics, and related fields are encouraged to apply. Please contact us by email and indicate [Undergraduate Research] or [Intern] in the subject line.
