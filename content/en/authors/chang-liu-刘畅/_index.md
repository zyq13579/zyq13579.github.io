Chang Liu is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Advanced Manufacturing and Robotics at College of Engineering, Peking University, China. He was a Postdoctoral Associate at the School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, Cornell University. He has also worked at Nvidia Corporation and Ford Motor Company. His research is focused on perception, planning, and control of intelligent robotics systems on both individual and large-scale levels for mission-critical situations.
